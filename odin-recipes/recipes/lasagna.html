Description

Lasagna one of the most innovative, Italian cuisines consisting of creamy tomato sauce with layers of meat and pasta

Ingredients

  • Creamy Ricotta Mixture
  • Savory Meat sauce (Marinara)
  • Spinach
  • Pasta

Steps

  1. Boil and cook your pasta until Al dente
  2. Prepare your Meat sauce -- Cook your meat and mix your Marinara sauce
  3. Combine Cheese mixture -- Stir the cheese mixture together
  4. Layer & Bake with the noodles on the bottom, then the meat, then some sauce and bake until top is golden brown
